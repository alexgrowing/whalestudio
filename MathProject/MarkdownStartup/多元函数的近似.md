## 切平面

### 如何表示空间中的一个平面
$经过(x_0,y_0,z_0)的平面L(x,y)=a(x-x_0)+b(y-y_0)+z_0$
### 计算切平面
$对于图形f(x,y),在点(x_0,y_0,f(x_0,y_0))其切平面要符合下列条件\\1.L(x,y)=a(x-x_0)+b(y-y_0)+f(x_0,y_0)\\2.a=\dfrac{\partial{f}}{\partial{x}}(x_0,y_0)\\3.b=\dfrac{\partial{f}}{\partial{y}}(x_0,y_0)$

$$求f(x,y)=3-\dfrac{1}{3}x^2-y^2在(1,-2)对应位置上的点的切平面$$
>$解:\\f(1,-2)=3-\dfrac{1}{3}1^2-(-2)^2=-\dfrac{4}{3}\\\quad\\a=\dfrac{\partial{f}}{\partial{x}}(1,-2)=-\dfrac{2}{3}(1)=-\dfrac{2}{3}\\\quad\\b=\dfrac{\partial{f}}{\partial{y}}(1,-2)=-2(-2)=4\\\quad\\L(x,y)=-\dfrac{2}{3}(x-1)+4(y+2)-\dfrac{4}{3}$

### 局部线性化(向量表达式)
$对于f(x,y)在(x_0,y_0)位置上的局部线性化\\L_f(x,y)=f_x(x_0,y_0)(x-x_0)+f_y(x_0,y_0)(y-y_0)+f(x_0,y_0)\\\quad\\设X=\begin{bmatrix}x\\y\end{bmatrix},X_0=\begin{bmatrix}x_0\\y_0\end{bmatrix}\\\quad\\L_f(X)=f(X_0)+\nabla{f}(X_0)\cdot(X-X_0)$

## 二次逼近
$二次逼近是比局部线性化对原函数有更高精度的近似\\\quad\\L(x,y)=a+bx+cy\\\quad\\Q(x,y)=a+bx+cy+dx^2+exy+fy^2$

### 二次逼近公式
$Q(x,y)要符合下列条件\\1.Q(x,y)=f(x_0,y_0)+f_x(x_0,y_0)(x-x_0)+f_y(x_0,y_0)(y-y_0)+a(x-x_0)^2+b(x-x_0)(y-y_0)+c(y-y_0)^2\\\quad\\2.\dfrac{\partial^2Q}{\partial{x^2}}(x_0,y_0)=\dfrac{\partial^2f}{\partial{x^2}}(x_0,y_0)\\\quad\\3.\dfrac{\partial^2Q}{\partial{y^2}}(x_0,y_0)=\dfrac{\partial^2f}{\partial{y^2}}(x_0,y_0)\\\quad\\4.\dfrac{\partial^2Q}{\partial{y}\partial{x}}(x_0,y_0)=\dfrac{\partial^2f}{\partial{y}\partial{x}}(x_0,y_0)$

$\dfrac{\partial^2{Q}}{\partial{x^2}}=\dfrac{\partial}{\partial{x}}(0+f_x(x_0,y_0)+0+2a(x-x_0)+b(y-y_0)+0)=2a=f_{xx}(x_0,y_0)\\则a=\dfrac{1}{2}f_{xx}(x_0,y_0)\\\quad\\\dfrac{\partial^2Q}{\partial{y}\partial{x}}=b=f_{xy}(x_0,y_0)\\\quad\\\dfrac{\partial^2Q}{\partial{y^2}}=2c=f_{yy}(x_0,y_0)$

$Q(x,y)=f(x_0,y_0)+f_x(x_0,y_0)(x-x_0)+f_y(x_0,y_0)(y-y_0)+\dfrac{1}{2}f_{xx}(x_0,y_0)(x-x_0)^2+f_{xy}(x_0,y_0)(x-x_0)(y-y_0)+\dfrac{1}{2}f_{yy}(x_0,y_0)(y-y_0)^2$

$$求f(x,y)=e^{x/2}\cdot \sin(y)在(0,\pi/2)的二次逼近函数Q(x,y)$$
>$解:\\f(0,\pi/2)=1\\f_x=\dfrac{1}{2}e^{x/2}\cdot \sin(y);f_x(0,\pi/2)=1/2\\f_y=e^{x/2}\cdot \cos(y);f_y(0,\pi/2)=0\\f_{xx}=\dfrac{1}{4}e^{x/2}\cdot\sin(y);f_{xx}(0,\pi/2)=1/4\\f_{xy}=\dfrac{1}{2}e^{x/2}\cdot\cos(y);f_{xy}(0,\pi/2)=0\\f_{yy}=-e^{x/2}\cdot\sin(y);f_{yy}(0,\pi/2)=-1\\quad\\Q(x,y)=1+\dfrac{1}{2}(x-0)+0(y-\pi/2)+\dfrac{1}{2}\dfrac{1}{4}(x-0)^2+0(x-0)(y-\pi/2)+\dfrac{1}{2}(-1)(y-\pi/2)^2=1+\dfrac{1}{2}x+\dfrac{1}{8}x^2-\dfrac{1}{2}(y-\dfrac{\pi}{2})^2$

### 黑塞矩阵
$f(x,y)=e^{x/2}\sin(y)\\H_f=\begin{bmatrix}\dfrac{\partial^2f}{\partial{x^2}}&\dfrac{\partial^2f}{\partial{x}\partial{y}}\\\\\dfrac{\partial^2f}{\partial{y}\partial{x}}&\dfrac{\partial^2f}{\partial{y^2}}\end{bmatrix}=\begin{bmatrix}\dfrac{1}{4}e^{x/2}\sin(y)&\dfrac{1}{2}e^{x/2}cos(y)\\\\\dfrac{1}{2}e^{x/2}cos(y)&-e^{x/2}\sin(y)\end{bmatrix}$

$f(x,y,z)的H_f=\begin{bmatrix}\dfrac{\partial^2f}{\partial{x^2}}&\dfrac{\partial^2f}{\partial{x}\partial{y}}&\dfrac{\partial^2f}{\partial{x}\partial{z}}\\\\\dfrac{\partial^2f}{\partial{y}\partial{x}}&\dfrac{\partial^2f}{\partial{y^2}}&\dfrac{\partial^2f}{\partial{y}\partial{z}}\\\\\dfrac{\partial^2f}{\partial{z}\partial{x}}&\dfrac{\partial^2f}{\partial{z}\partial{y}}&\dfrac{\partial^2f}{\partial{z^2}}\end{bmatrix}$

### 二次逼近的向量形式
$ax+by+cz=\begin{bmatrix}a\\b\\c\end{bmatrix}\cdot\begin{bmatrix}x\\y\\z\end{bmatrix}=V\cdot X$

$ax^2+2bxy+cy^2=\begin{bmatrix}x&y\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}a&b\\b&c\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}x\\y\end{bmatrix}=X^TMX$

>$三元二次型\\X^TMX=\begin{bmatrix}x&y&z\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}a&b&c\\b&d&e\\c&e&f\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}x\\y\\z\end{bmatrix}$

$对于f(X)=f(\begin{bmatrix}x\\y\end{bmatrix})在位置X_0=\begin{bmatrix}x_0\\y_0\end{bmatrix}上的二次逼近为\\Q(X)=f(X_0)+\nabla{f}(X_0)\cdot(X-X_0)+\dfrac{1}{2}(X-X_0)^TH_f(X_0)(X-X_0)$

## 多元函数的最值

### 平行与XY轴的切平面
$\dfrac{\partial{f}}{\partial{x}}(x_0,y_0)=0\quad \&\&\quad \dfrac{\partial{f}}{\partial{y}}(x_0,y_0)=0\\\quad\\\begin{bmatrix}\dfrac{\partial{f}}{\partial{x}}\\\\\dfrac{\partial{f}}{\partial{y}}\end{bmatrix}=\begin{bmatrix}0\\\\0\end{bmatrix}\\\quad\\\nabla{f}=\vec{0}$

### 鞍点
$f(x,y)=x^2-y^2在(0,0)位置\nabla{f}=\vec{0},但不是局部最值\\从x方向看是局部最小值,但是从y方向看是局限最大值$

### 二阶偏导检验法
$对于f(x,y),如果\nabla{f}(x_0,y_0)=0\\H=f_{xx}(x_0,y_0)f_{yy}(x_0,y_0)-f_{xy}(x_0,y_0)^2\begin{cases}(x_0,y_0)为极大或极小值&H>0\\(x_0,y_0)为鞍点&H<0\\无法判断&H=0\end{cases}$

$$求f(x,y)=x^4-4x^2+y^2的极大极小值$$
>$\nabla{f}=\begin{bmatrix}\dfrac{\partial{f}}{\partial{x}}\\\\\dfrac{\partial{f}}{\partial{y}}\end{bmatrix}=\begin{bmatrix}4x^3-8x\\\\2y\end{bmatrix}=\begin{bmatrix}0\\\\0\end{bmatrix}\\\quad\\\therefore\begin{cases}x_1=0,x_2=\sqrt{2},x_3=-\sqrt{2}\\y=0\end{cases}\\\quad\\\therefore解为(0,0),(\sqrt{2},0),(-\sqrt{2},0)$
>
>$\dfrac{\partial^2{f}}{\partial{x^2}}=12x^2-8\\\quad\\\begin{cases}\dfrac{\partial^2{f}}{\partial{x^2}}(0,0)=-8,是x方向上的局部最大值\\\\\dfrac{\partial^2{f}}{\partial{x^2}}(\sqrt{2},0)=16,是x方向上的局部最小值\\\\\dfrac{\partial^2{f}}{\partial{x^2}}(-\sqrt{2},0)=16,是x方向上的局部最小值\end{cases}\\\quad\\\dfrac{\partial^2{f}}{\partial{y^2}}=2,对于上述三个位置在y方向上都是局部最小值\\\quad\\\dfrac{\partial^2f}{\partial{y}\partial{x}}=0\\\quad\\H=\begin{cases}-8\cdot2-0^2=-16<0&(0,0)\\16\cdot 2-0^2=32>0&(\sqrt{2},0)\\16\cdot 2-0^2=32>0&(-\sqrt{2},0)\end{cases}$
